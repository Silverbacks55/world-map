About

About this tool

The Super Pollutant Corporate Estimator matches your company profile against a database of super pollutant regulations, incentive programs, and voluntary carbon market projects. The matching is based on your headquarters country, source and export countries, industry sector, and revenue band.

Data sources

Regulations data is sourced and maintained by GBB Strategy. The database currently covers 908 regulations across 212 countries and jurisdictions. Biomethane program data and VCM project data are maintained separately and updated on an ongoing basis. Pollutant exposure profiles are derived from the EPA Inventory of U.S. Greenhouse Gas Emissions and Sinks 1990–2022 (EPA 430-R-24-004, April 2024) and the EPA Greenhouse Gas Reporting Program (GHGRP) 2023.

Limitations

This tool is intended as a high-level directional guide only. It does not constitute legal or compliance advice. Regulations change frequently and coverage varies by country. Industry flag tagging is ongoing — results for some sectors and countries may be incomplete. Always verify applicability with a qualified compliance professional.

Privacy

No data entered into this tool is stored or transmitted to GBB Strategy. All processing occurs locally in your browser.

Methodology

Matching methodology

Regulations are matched to your profile using the following logic:

  • Domestic: Regulations in your HQ country whose pollutant profile overlaps with your sector's primary super pollutants.
  • Imports: Regulations in your source countries using the same pollutant matching logic.
  • Exports: Regulations in your export countries using the same pollutant matching logic.
  • Incentives: Biomethane and renewable gas incentive programs in any of your countries.
  • VCM Projects: Voluntary carbon market projects in your countries whose project type is associated with your sector's pollutants.
